Digital Parking Meter

May, 2020

A touch screen parking meter that allows drivers to pay parking with cash, debit/credit card and parking attendant to spot parking violations easily.

Constraints

  1. The device needs to accomadate 6 parking spots.
  2. The device is not connected to the internet or any bluetooth device.
  3. The device has a single touchscreen with no zoom, hard touch and advanced touchscreen functionality supported.

Design Procress

1. Identifying User Goals

There are 2 main user types: Drivers and Parking attendant

Drivers' Goals

Icons made by Nikita Golubev from www.flaticon.com
  • Add time to the correct spot
  • Extend their time
  • See how much time is left
  • Pay with cash or credit/debit
  • Print out receipts

Parking attendant's Goals

Icons made by Flat Icons from www.flaticon.com
  • Spots out parking violations

2. Analysing Current Solution

Drivers' Frustrations

  • Not really sure if they need to pay when it's outside paying hours or during weekends
  • Instruction are unclear sometimes. Not sure if select time first or swpit card or insert coins

Parking attendants' Frustrations

  • To spot out parking violation, they need to go up to each car and try to find the reciept on their dashboard
  • Sometime there is multiple reciept and takes time to identify the right one

Solution

Followed the design language of the app to minimize changes the development team need to make. Online Clickable Protoype
